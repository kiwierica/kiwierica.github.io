One Hour Reading-Day 1

From Harry Potter and Philosopher’s Stone

Mr. Dursley might have been drifting into an uneasy sleep.

drift: 状态的改变 to gradually change from being in one condition, situation etc into another without realising it.
Eg. She was just drifting into sleep when the alarm went off.
He drifted in and out of conciousness. 他神志时而清醒时而迷糊。
drift off to gradually fall asleep. 慢慢入睡
He felt himself drifting off to sleep.
I was just drifting off when the phone rang.

Henry Huggins with Ribsy

Henry Huggins is a third grade boy who lives on Klickitat Street with his parents. He always wishes something exciting happen in his life because every day for him is ordinary and nothing interesting except for his tonsils out.

Until one Wednesday afternoon, after he swam at Y.M.C.A for an hour, something special happened. Henry bought a chocolate ice cream cone in a drugstore and enjoyed his ice cream. Suddenly a skinny dog came into his eyes, waggling his long and thin tail and waiting for a bite of ice cream. Henry called him “Ribsy”, for the dog was so thin that his ribs showed right through his skin.

Henry dreamed of having a dog before, and it seemed a good chance to keep this dog. He planned to take Ribsy home. Before that, he had to call his mother to tell her his decision. Mrs Huggins didn’t want her son to keep the dog but she didn’t have a strong reason to refuse her son. So she suggested if Henry could take the dog home, then he could keep Ribsy.

Henry failed to take the first bus becuase the driver refused him and his dog because of the rule of no dogs on the bus, unless the boy found a box to put his dog in. Henry lifted off the bus with Ribsy and went back to the drugstore for help. But the clert only found a cardbox without lid for him. The second time Henry took on the bus with Ribsy in the box. Unfortunately, the bus driver refused him again becuase the box didn’t have a lid that can keep the dog insided it. Again, he had to lift off to find another solution.

Build Your Own Blog

To be honest, as a developer,I rarely write blogs particularly about technology which makes me feel ashamed. Serveral months ago, I built a personal blog based on github and Jekyll. But I feel Jekyll is a little tricky and not convenient or maybe I don’t know it well. Until recently I found another tool called “Hexo” which can build a personal blog easily, so I decided to turn to Hexo to build and mantain this blog.

About Hexo

Hexo official website: https://hexo.io/
Hexo is new to me too. It’s a light blog framework based on Node.js. Therefore, if you want to use Hexo to build your blog, you need to install node.js on your computer first.

Steps to setup your blog

  • install node.js
  • install npm
  • intall hexo
  • register gitub
  • setup github
  • create new repository from github
  • init hexo locally
  • deploy onto github

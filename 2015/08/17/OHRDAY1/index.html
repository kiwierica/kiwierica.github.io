One Hour Reading-Day 1

  • English
  • OHR

From Harry Potter and Philosopher’s Stone

Mr. Dursley might have been drifting into an uneasy sleep.

drift: 状态的改变 to gradually change from being in one condition, situation etc into another without realising it.
Eg. She was just drifting into sleep when the alarm went off.
He drifted in and out of conciousness. 他神志时而清醒时而迷糊。
drift off to gradually fall asleep. 慢慢入睡
He felt himself drifting off to sleep.
I was just drifting off when the phone rang.

Harry picked it up and stared at it, his heart twanging like a giant elastic band.

一个很有意思的比喻，后半句用了独立主格值得借鉴。

His face went from read to green faster than a set of traffic lights. And it didn’t stop there. Within seconds it was greyish white of old porridge.

罗琳用这个比喻太有才了。德思礼脸色变得跟红绿灯一样快，这还没完，几秒钟成了灰白色的老粥。 神比喻。

He took both Harry and Dudley by the scruffs of their necks
揪着脖子。

Harry could see Uncle Vernon’s shiny black shoes pacing up and down the kicken.

pacing up and down 踱步，徘徊；不知道为何是up and down

But Uncle Vernon was tearing the letters into pieces before his eyes. He stayed at home and nailed up the mail slot.

Henry Huggins with Ribsy

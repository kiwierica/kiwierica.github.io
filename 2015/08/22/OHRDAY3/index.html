One Hour Reading-Day 3

  • English
  • OHR

On Harry’s 11th Birthday, Hagrid came to an island where Uncle Vernon escaped. On a severe night, Hagrid came to visit Harry, bringing the letter and a birthday cake to Harry. He took Harry to the wizard’s world, buying all things for the first year in Hogwards. Harry got to know himself, where he came from and who his parents were.

Harry felt the warmth wash over him as though he’d sunk into a hot bath.

sink into a hot bath

The giant sat back down on the sofa, which sagged under his weight.

He had leapt to his feet.

leap to one’s feet:跳起来

Term begins on September 1. We await your owl by no later than July 31.

by no later than:不晚于

Swore we’d stamp it out of him.

发誓和他一刀两断

Hagrid suddenly pulled out a very dirty, spotted handkerchief and blew his nose with a sound like a foghorn.

He cast one last terrified look at Hagrid and slammed the door behind him.
…but I suppose he was so much like a pig anyway there wasn’t much left to do.

上面这句话实在很幽默，达利胖的跟猪一样，海格不需要再多为他做些什么了，装个尾巴足矣像猪了。

Harry scarmbled to his feet.
爬起来，类似的还有leap to his feet，跳起来。

The sea gleamed in the sunlight.

…clapping his great hand on Harry’s shoulder and making Harry’s knees buckle.

海格太有利了，把哈利拍的膝盖都打弯了。

Then there was a great scraping of chairs and the next moment, Harry found himself shaking hands with everyone in the Leaky Cauldron.

酒吧的人都从椅子上站起来与哈利握手，所以椅子有摩擦声。
The chairs scraped. 椅子移动发出的摩擦声。

I’m all of a flutter.

but then he took a year off to get some firsthand experience…

Harry’s head was swimming.

哈利有点摸不着头脑，毫无头绪。

He turned his head in every direction as they walked up the street, trying to look at everything at once.

哈利在对角巷大开眼界了。 an eye-opened experience

Several boys of about Harry’s age had their noses pressed against a window with broomsticks in it.

Hagrid did look very green.

估计海格晕车吧，green这里就是ill的意思。

They were going even deeper now and gathering speed.

车子加速可以说gather speed.
